About me

Get a closer look at who I am!

It's me, Federica!
A junior front end developer and UX design enthusiast from Italy, currently looking for a position abroad to work in a company where I can grow professionally.
But first let's take a step back! In 2019 I finished my studies in Art management and after a wonderful experience as an intern at the Manchester Art Gallery I realised that wasn't the path for me despite my great passion for art.
Gradually I discovered a great interest in digital so I studied and deepened the aspects related to websites and apps.

But it was a simple basic coding course in the summer of 2020 that made me fall in love with web development and I've never stopped since!
After a few weeks I found a position as an intern in a web agency where I worked on digital marketing and web development. However, I have never stopped studying and continue to acquire more skills and create new projects!
